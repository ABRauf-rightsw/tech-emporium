Help & Support FAQ

Common questions regarding shipment processes, payment options, local warranties, and hardware returns.

We offer flat-rate delivery charges of Rs. 250 across Pakistan. For orders totaling above Rs. 10,000, shipping is entirely FREE. Deliveries within Karachi take 24-48 hours. Orders to Lahore, Islamabad, and Rawalpindi take 2-3 business days. Remote locations might require up to 4-5 business days.

We accept various payment methods:
  • Cash On Delivery (COD) for orders up to Rs. 150,000
  • Direct Bank Transfer (Bank Alfalah / HBL)
  • Easypaisa Mobile Wallet
  • JazzCash Mobile Wallet
For high-value corporate orders above Rs. 150,000, bank transfer verification is required before shipping.

All laptops, processors, and components sold at Tech Emporium come with original manufacturer warranty cards (e.g., 1 Year Brand Warranty). You can claim warranty directly at official service centers (Dell, HP, Lenovo, ASUS) in Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, or send the hardware back to us to manage the claiming process on your behalf.

We support a 7-Day Replacement Policy if your hardware arrives with manufacturing defects, shipping damages, or mismatching product models. The item must be returned in its original, unopened packaging box with all warranty labels intact. Open box software issues or changes of mind are not eligible for replacement.

Once your order is processed from our warehouse, you will receive a tracking ID via Email/SMS. You can audit order progress directly on our Order History page or enter the courier ID on Leopards Courier or TCS logistics portal websites.
